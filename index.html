Find a charging station!

With over 17500 stations across the country, an EV charger is closer than you might realize.
Planning a road trip? No problem, PlugHubs has you covered.
Just enter in your starting and ending locations and the app will show you all the ev charging stations along the way.
Or, if you find yourself in a new place, find the nearest one by geolocation or by entering in the address or just zip code.
